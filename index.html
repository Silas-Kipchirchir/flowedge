Who We Are

FlowEdge Industrial Solution Ltd is a Kenya-registered, founder-led industrial company providing sourcing, supply, and local representation support for industrial flow-control and process equipment. Our focus is on technical alignment, responsive communication, and reliable coordination to deliver projects efficiently and reliably. We build long-term partnerships tailored to local market needs.

What We Do

We specialize in the sourcing and supply of industrial mechanical systems, with a strong focus on equipment that controls, moves, and contains fluids and energy within process environments. Our work is centered on practical, field-proven solutions backed by trusted manufacturers.
